Automation

With agile development environments and rapid release cycles, Automation is more crucial to an organization then ever before. With automated tests you can do rapid validation of software changes. Automation provides greater coverage, consistency, faster turn around time and eliminates time constraints associated with manual testing. Test automation also benefits QA engineers by freeing them to set up complex system test scenarios for new features that is not fully baked and not ready for automation.
QAPulse's automation team can help you incorporate automation into your product lifecycle that can generate sustainable ROI and savings in time and money. Our test developers are experienced with building a variety of automation tools such as:

  • Selenium
  • Watir
  • Cucumber
  • Rational
  • SOASTA
  • and many others
