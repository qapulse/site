Services

We provide wide range of software testing services that span across all phases of software development. Our on shore/off shore delivery model ensure clients get predictable software quality benefits with minimum time and cost investment associated with testing activities. QAPulse team will work as an integrated part of your team and help you identify your needs, recommend appropriate solutions, and implement it to achieve our mutual goal of delivering highest quality software. Here's a list of testing services, in addition to services we also provide QA staff augmentation services:

  • Functional We offer a wide range functional testing services for web, mobile or desktop application. Our services include writing test cases, execution of test cases, exploratory testing and test automation. So whether you're looking for bugs, an expert tester to write, execute or automate test cases we can help.
  • Automation QAPulse's automation team can help you incorporate automation into your product lifecycle that can generate sustainable ROI and savings in time and money. Our test developers are experienced with popular automation frameworks.
  • Load/Performance In today's age organizations need to test their web, mobile and desktop applications faster, more rigorously, and at web scale. Our performance lab can help you build performance test specification, execute it, identify weakest link and help you tune your application.
